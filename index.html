Welcome to Petoskey Stone Tile & More

Our Petoskey Stone products can be utilized in numerous ways to give your interior a very unique look and visual experience. Visualize your shower walls, floors, insert details, kitchen back splash, bar top, Fireplace surround, and office space, with either an insert of Petoskey Stone Tile or the entire surface covered.

Our hand crafted tiles will enhance most designs, or can be added to future remodeling projects. The cutting and polishing is done in Northern Michigan and can be shipped to anywhere in the world.

Petoskey Stone Tile and More strives to meet customers needs and offers finished inventory ready to fulfill orders promptly, without “make to order” time delays.

We look forward to taking your interior design to the next level of custom.
